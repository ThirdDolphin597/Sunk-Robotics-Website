Sunk Robotics is a high school, student-run robotics team based in Medford Vocational Technical High School, Massachusetts. Since our founding in November 2021, we have developed multiple underwater engineering solutions and competed in the MATE ROV Competition.

Our mission is to make reliable, robust, and reputable underwater engineering solutions while inspiring the next generation of engineers and innovators.
